About LitMatch

Igniting a lifelong passion for reading in every child. We believe the right book can change a life.

Our Story

LitMatch started with a simple idea: every child deserves to experience the joy of getting lost in a good book. We saw that many young readers struggled not because they disliked reading, but because they hadn't found the "right" books for them—stories that reflected their interests, sparked their curiosity, and matched their reading level.

We set out to bridge this gap. By combining a personal touch with smart technology, we created a platform to deliver personalized book recommendations that truly resonate with young readers. Our goal is to transform reading from a chore into a cherished adventure.

Our Mission

To foster a lifelong love of reading in youth by providing personalized book recommendations that inspire, educate, and entertain.

Our Vision

A world where every child sees themselves as a reader and has access to stories that open up new worlds of possibility.

Our Values

Personalization, Empathy, Community, and the magical power of a good story. We are committed to making reading accessible and fun for everyone.
